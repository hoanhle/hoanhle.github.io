Hoanh Le

Hi, I’m Hoanh. I like building cool stuff with smart people.

My background is a ML/AI Engineer with 5+ years of experience in computer vision, specializing in bringing real-time ML products to production. I’m currently working at an A.I unicorn in Zurich, Switzerland, under Lukas Bossard (prev Luis Haug, Mattia Desana). When not at work, I ideally like to spend time on my research in the Deep Learning Research group under the supervision of Sam Spilsbury and Alexander Ilin.

At Aalto University, I’m pursuing my master’s in Machine Learning, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence with a minor in Financial Engineering. You can check out my talk here. I completed my bachelor’s thesis on studying the effect of Spatial Transformation Network within PointNet at the Computer Vision lab in Tampere University, where I provided empirical evidence that it doesn’t work, under the supervision of Joni-Kristian Kämäräinen.

Before that, I dedicated part of my time to advanced mathematics at Hanoi Amsterdam High School for the Gifted and the rest to club activities with the Society of Open Science.

In my free time nowadays, I read books, go to the gym, play tennis, and travel.

Thanks for visiting, feel free to reach out to me at hoanh.le@aalto.fi. I’m always open to interesting conversations and collaboration.
